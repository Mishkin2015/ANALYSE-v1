About

ANALYSE is learning analytics tool developed for Open edX. This is a beta release which extends the learning analytics functionality of Open edX with 12 new visualizations. A new tab has been addded in the course dashboard to access ANALYSE. ANALYSE has been developed in the Gradient lab, which is the e-learning laboratory inside the GAST group, as a part of the Department of Telematic Engineering, at the Universidad Carlos III of Madrid (Spain).
